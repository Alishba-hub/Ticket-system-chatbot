General support knowledge base:
- You can reach us via email, live chat, or phone during working hours.
- Our support hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday (excluding public holidays).
- We aim to respond to all support tickets within 24 business hours, though response times may vary depending on volume.
- For self-service help, please visit our Help Center which contains detailed step-by-step guides.
- Support agents must not make guarantees about resolution timelines.
- Agents should never provide technical advice outside documented procedures.
- If unsure or if the issue involves another team, agents must inform the user that the request is being escalated or reviewed.
- Support agents should always maintain a polite, helpful, and professional tone.
